About College

Aabasaheb Shivajirao Sitaram Patil Institute of Polytechnic (ASSPIP), Jalgaon was established in 2010 and is a reputed technical institute located at Chinchpura, Taluka Dharangaon, District Jalgaon, Maharashtra.

The institute is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It is committed to providing quality technical education and developing skilled professionals for industry.

The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, computer facilities, sports grounds, gymnasium, auditorium, canteen, health center, and adequate parking facilities for students and staff.

The institute offers three full-time diploma programs:

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

Each diploma program has an approved intake of 60 students, making a total intake capacity of 180 students. The institute focuses on academic excellence, practical learning, and preparing students to become industry-ready professionals.

ASSPIP follows a transparent admission process and encourages students to achieve excellence through quality education, innovation, and overall personality development.
